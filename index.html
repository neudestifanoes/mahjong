Leadership

These are the executive positions that will be held by elected memebers every semester

President

Leads meetings and events, coordinates organizational tasks, delegates to the executive board, and initiates new ideas.

Vice President

Supports the president, builds campus connections, and leads meetings in the president's absence.

Treasurer

Maintains financial records, prepares reports, manages the budget, and leads meetings in the absence of the President and Vice President.

Event Coordinator

Manages meeting spaces, supports fellow officers, and leads meetings when others are absent.

Public Relations Officer

Handles club advertising, aids in community outreach, and leads meetings in the absence of other officers.

Electors

All active members are eligible to vote and run for executive positions. To become active, attend events and pay membership dues.
